# Privacy Policy for Awn App

Awn App takes your privacy seriously. This privacy policy explains how we collect and use your personal information to provide a better service experience.

## Collection of Information
To ensure fast and accurate service without errors, we collect:
* **Contact Information:** We collect your phone number and name to manage your orders and contact you.
* **Precise Location Data:** We collect your GPS location to help our team reach your address quickly and avoid any delivery mistakes. This includes background location access to provide real-time updates even when the app is closed.
* **Device Information:** We track basic information like device type to optimize app performance.

## Data Usage
Your location data is used strictly for service and delivery purposes. We do not share your personal data with third parties for advertising.

## Security
